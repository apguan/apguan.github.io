Pride

Pride is a project that I worked on to help Teach for America tackle the challenge teachers face when creating lesson plans. It uses IBM's Watson to help parse through data and allows teachers to give feedback to one another. It won not only Best Education Tool, but also Best Use of Cisco Spark at HackDavis. Currently, we are revamping the project to be a MEAN stack project. The goal is to create and open-source app to help teachers provide the best learning experience possible.
